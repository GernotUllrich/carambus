Three E2E scenarios + 1 control + 1 security probe:

   drive both players to result=80 in 30 innings, verify modal opens with radio
   fieldset, pick A, verify GameParticipation.points reflects A=win / B=lost.
2. **TR-B BK-2 Nachstoss-Ballziel-Parität:** start a BK-2 match, drive Anstoss to
   balls_goal=70 in inning 5, drive Nachstoss to balls_goal=70 in his Nachstoss-Aufnahme
   (inning 6), verify Plan 02's end_of_set fix fires, modal opens, pick B, verify
   `finish_match!` fired and `game.data['tiebreak_winner']=='playerb'`.

   `tiebreak_on_draw` set, drive to a tied result, verify modal opens WITHOUT radio
   fieldset, click "Ergebnis bestätigen", verify legacy draw flow.

   reflex via Capybara JavaScript injection bypassing the radio (e.g. `page.execute_script`
   to manually invoke the StimulusReflex without form data) → verify the modal stays
   open and game state did NOT advance.
5. **Human-verify checkpoint:** operator validates the visual UX, language, modal
   spacing, dark-mode contrast, mobile viewport rendering.
