### Task 2: Controller CLAMP + service + migration

- `app/controllers/table_monitors_controller.rb`:
  - Removed `:set_target_points` from params whitelist; added `:balls_goal`.
  - Quick-game BK block triggers on `bk2_kombi` OR `bk_family` `quick_game_form` values.

  - ZERO occurrences of `set_target_points` remain in controller.
- `app/services/table_monitor/game_setup.rb`: `BK_NAME_TO_FORM` constant added; `derive_free_game_form` name-fallback now maps all 5 BK-* names instead of just `BK2-Kombi`.
- `db/migrate/20260425065047_backfill_bk2_balls_goal.rb`: Idempotent backfill migration; copies `bk2_options["set_target_points"]` to `tournament_monitor.balls_goal` where `balls_goal` is absent/zero. `disable_ddl_transaction!`. Ran cleanly (0 records backfilled on dev DB).
