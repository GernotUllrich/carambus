## Regression Observations (non-findings, for verifier)

1. **Controller `start_game` remains complex** (~280 lines including
   param processing and branch logic). The BK2-Kombi branches are
   well-commented but the overall method is a candidate for extraction into
   a form-objects-per-discipline pattern. Out of plan 06 scope; noted for
   the god-object refactoring initiative described in CLAUDE.md.

2. **`discipline_a` / `discipline_b` hidden fields are double-submitted**
   from `scoreboard_free_game_karambol_new.html.erb:183-184` (always emitted)
   AND from the karambol `radio_select` partial at line 310/334. Rails
   takes the last-submitted value; when BK2 is selected, Alpine sets the
   hidden field to `"BK2-Kombi"` but the radio_select (which is visually
   hidden via Alpine `x-show`, not removed from DOM) still submits a
   numeric index. This would be a bug except that the controller's T-38.1-06-01
   CLAMP at `table_monitors_controller.rb:215-216` hard-assigns
   `"BK2-Kombi"` whenever `free_game_form == "bk2_kombi"`, making the
   form-level ambiguity moot. No action required — the CLAMP is the
   correct defence — but worth documenting for future maintainers who
   might be tempted to remove either the hidden field or the CLAMP.

3. **Test coverage is proportionate.** Controller tests a-f exercise the
   BK2 detail path, BK2 quick path, T-38.1-06-01 CLAMP, and karambol/
   pool/snooker regressions. System test additionally covers DOM render,
   AdvanceMatchState integration path (including D-15 2-point rule and
   idempotency), and the navigation/post path. The deferred detail-form
   Capybara test is correctly covered by controller test 'b' per the
   plan's fallback clause.
