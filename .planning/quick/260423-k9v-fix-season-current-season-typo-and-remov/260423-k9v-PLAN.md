Purpose: The class-level `@year` / `@current_season` assignments on lines 25-26 run exactly ONCE at class-definition time (when Rails autoloads the file). Output: A cleaner `Season.current_season` that does a plain no-cache lookup with an update_seasons fallback. No behavioral change from the caller's perspective.
