## üÜò Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### During the Match

**Q: I entered an incorrect point. How do I correct it?**  
A: Use the undo button (‚Ü∂) in the scoreboard. You can reverse the last actions.

**Q: The scoreboard is not responding. What to do?**  
A: Refresh the page in the browser (F5 or swipe-down). Your data will be preserved.

**Q: My match is not on the scoreboard.**  
A: Contact the tournament manager. The match may need to be started first.

**Q: Can I enter points without a touch screen?**  
A: Yes! You can also use keyboard or mouse. Numbers for points, Enter to confirm.

### Tournaments & Schedule

**Q: Where do I find my schedule?**  
A: Go to the tournament page and click on "Schedule". Or use search: "My matches today".

**Q: When is my next match?**  
A: On the home page under "Upcoming Matches" or in the tournament monitor.

**Q: My opponent is not here. What to do?**  
A: Report to the tournament manager. They can postpone or score the match.

**Q: Can I see who is currently playing?**  
A: Yes, on the tournament monitor or the tournament overview under "Running Matches".

### Statistics & Rankings

**Q: Where do I find my statistics?**  
A: Under "My Profile" ‚Üí "Statistics" or use search: "My statistics".

**Q: Why is my average incorrect?**  
A: The average is calculated over all rated matches. Training matches don't count.

**Q: Can I download statistics?**  
A: Yes, as PDF or CSV via the export button in statistics.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Do I need an account?**  
A: For result entry at scoreboard: No. For personal statistics and profile: Yes.

**Q: Does it work without internet?**  
A: Yes, in the local club network. Internet is required for external access.

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**  
A: All modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). Recommended: Chrome/Chromium.

**Q: Is there an app?**  
A: Not separately at the moment. The web application works like an app and can be added to the home screen.

## üí° Tips & Tricks

### Efficient Point Entry

**Carom**:
- Use quick entry: Just type the number
- At end of inning: Switch immediately to next player
- High run is automatically recognized

**Pool**:
- Ball pocketed: Tap on the ball (faster than counter)
- Foul: Use foul button for automatic ball-in-hand

**Snooker**:
- Use color buttons in sequence
- Break mode: Points are automatically added
- On foul: Opponent gets points automatically

### Mobile Devices

- **Landscape mode**: Rotate device for better overview
- **Home screen**: Add page to home screen for quick access
- **Fullscreen**: Use fullscreen mode for distraction-free display

### Statistics

- **Filter**: Use filters for specific time periods or disciplines
- **Comparisons**: Compare yourself with other players via head-to-head
- **Export**: Download your data for your own analyses
